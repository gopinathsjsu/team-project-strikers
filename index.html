About

The Whitespring built in 1858 and named after White Sulphur Springs, the natural sulphur spring on the grounds, the Whitespring was famous for its Southern style. The Whitespring Resort had a long history of business with the federal government and counted 31 sitting U.S. presidents among its guests by 2077, 12 of them in the The Whitespring Presidential Cottage and Museum.
